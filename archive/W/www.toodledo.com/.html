What is Toodledo?

More than just a to-do list, Toodledo is a tool that lets you:

 • Make custom to do lists  • Create structured outlines  • Track your habits
 • Write long notes  • Collaborate with friends, family and your co-workers on goals and projects
All while safely stored online and synced across all your devices.
