Travel Insurance

Use the links on this page to quote and purchase international medical insurance and trip accident/cancelation insurance.

 

International Travel Medical Insurance This is a must-have for visitors relocating to the U.S. or to other countries. I have heard horror stories of huge medical bills incurred by those arriving here with no health insurance coverage. It is also useful for short or long term mission trips or for ex-patriots planning to stay overseas for extended periods. The below links will provide various coverage options in which you can get free instant quotes and even apply online:
  • HCC Medical

Trip Cancelation / Interruption Insurance This insurance was very useful to my family when we were removed from a flight (due to illness) when we had a layover in Hong Kong.  It is a must-have when booking a vacation such as a cruise, or family vacation.  This link will take you to a website allowing you to quote and purchase from a number of travel insurance companies. I’ve personally used TravelGuard, but there are also a number of other good companies.
