Oria is a free blogging theme, with a minimalist design. It has a full-width header and a featured slider while the posts are arranged in a grid style on the homepage. Oria’s design is simple and has a well-structured content, which makes it easier for the users to follow it.

Oria is a responsive masonry theme, with a clean look, parallax header image, social menu, custom colors, Google fonts, multilingual settings, a built-in featured posts carousel, an easy-to-use contact form, and lots of customization options.
