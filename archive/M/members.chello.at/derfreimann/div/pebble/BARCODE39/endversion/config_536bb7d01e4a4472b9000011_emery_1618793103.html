FAQ

How do I UPDATE the watchface?


ಠ◡ಠ

on iOS:
completely delete the watchface from your Pebble app, then look it up again in the app store and install it anew (=automatically most recent version)!

on Android:
select the watchface in the Pebble app and choose 'unload'. All you have to do now is reload it onto your Pebble... just out of the locker (=automatically most recent version)

ATTENTION: the date of the last update always refers to ANDROID, because there the updates will be published immediatey after release. On iOS however it could happen that it takes a couple of weeks, because iOS needs the JavaScript part of the watchface to be integrated into the Pebble app!
