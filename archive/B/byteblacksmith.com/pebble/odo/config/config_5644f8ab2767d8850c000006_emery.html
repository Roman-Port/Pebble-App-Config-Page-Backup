Battery Conservation
This watchface consumes power heavily while animating. Running with all battery savings turned off (When set to animate, "Fine" scrolling, and no sleeping) consumes roughly 30-40% battery per day. However, it also looks cool.

If you recharge your Pebble nightly, no need to worry. But to maximize your Pebble's battery life, use these controls to scale back on the animation as you find reasonable.
